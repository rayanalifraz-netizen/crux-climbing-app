## Description (4000 chars max)
Crux is the recovery tracker built for boulderers who train hard and want to stay injury-free.

Log your sessions, track how your body feels, and get a daily readiness score that tells you when to push and when to rest — based on your actual climbing data.

**DAILY CHECK-IN**
Rate soreness across 8 body zones and flag specific finger injuries. Crux calculates your Daily Readiness Score (DRS) so you know exactly where you stand each morning.

**SESSION LOGGING**
Log the grades you climbed, hold types (crimp, sloper, pinch, pocket), and movement styles. Crux calculates a Relative Effort Score (RES) that accounts for your personal max grade — not just absolute difficulty.

**CLIMBER HEALTH INDEX (CHI)**
A combined score tracking your load balance, recovery, and injury risk over the past 14 days. See your training trend at a glance.

**BODY HEATMAP**
A visual strain map showing which body parts are being loaded hardest across your recent sessions — so you can spot overuse before it becomes an injury.

**TRAINING CALENDAR**
A full history of your sessions and check-ins. Review past efforts and spot patterns in your training load.

**PROVIDER REPORT**
One-tap summary of your training load, recovery status, and body stress — formatted to share with your physio or coach.

**BUILT FOR PRIVACY**
All your data stays on your device. Optional cloud backup via Apple Sign In. No ads, no tracking.
